About C3 Green Energy

We are saving Energy

C3 Green Energy LLC was born out of a need to assist homeowners, businesses, and industry to finally have some control over rising energy costs. The company brings together years of practical experience and excellent customer service to assist customers in achieving maximum efficiency, while maintaining optimal performance. The catalyst for C3 Green Energy, LLC was the introduction of The Powerhouse power conditioning system by Black Hawk Energy Products, LLC. This revolutionary new energy savings system was the creation of electrician and inventor, Jerry Johnson. What started as a simple request from a senior citizen on a fixed income to help lower her monthly electric bill has evolved into an innovative product that provides significant energy savings and much more.
